Medway 2015 Schedule

The Medway Classic Regional Tournament welcomes competitors of all ages and belt ranks. Divisions will be created based on participants to ensure healthy competition in smaller rings. The tentative schedule will be available on March 15th.

The preliminary schedule is:

  • 10:00 Kata events:: ages 4 to 12 years
  • 11:00 sparring events: ages 4 to 12 years
  • 12:00 Weapons, all ranks
  • 1:00 Kata events 13 thru adult
  • 2:00 Sparring events 13 thru adult
  • Black Belt events will follow Immediately after these events.
